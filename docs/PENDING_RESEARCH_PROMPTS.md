## Prompt 1: Moovs Platform Deep-Dive

```
# Research Task: Moovs Platform Technical & Operational Analysis

## Objective
Conduct comprehensive due diligence on Moovs (moovs.app) as the primary
dispatch/booking software for LinkMia, a Miami airport transfer startup
with 2-3 drivers doing 50-200 trips/month.

## Research Areas

### 1. Pricing Validation
Confirm the pricing structure:
- Free tier: 10 rides/month - is this accurate?
- Pro plan: $199/month for 250 rides - confirm
- Setup fee: $299 - is it waived for startups?
- Are there ANY per-trip fees or hidden costs?
- What happens if we exceed 250 trips? Overage pricing?
- Annual discount available?
- Payment processing: Do they take a cut or just Stripe's 2.9%?

Search for:
- "Moovs pricing"
- "Moovs cost per trip"
- "Moovs vs Limo Anywhere pricing"
- Reddit/forums discussing Moovs costs

### 2. Technical Capabilities

**Flight Tracking**
- Does Moovs integrate with FlightAware or similar?
- Auto-adjust pickup time for delays?

**Driver App**
- iOS and Android availability?
- Offline mode for airport tunnels?
- GPS tracking reliability?
- Navigation integration (Waze, Google Maps)?

**Customer Experience**
- "Track My Ride" - how does it work?
- Automated SMS/email notifications?
- What triggers notifications?

**Dispatch & Scheduling**
- Calendar/Gantt view?
- Drag-and-drop trip assignment?
- Conflict detection?

### 3. Integration & API
- Does Moovs have an API?
- Can LinkMia's custom booking site push trips to Moovs?
- Webhook support for status updates?
- Zapier/Make integrations?

### 4. Developer & Operator Reviews
Search for real experiences:
- "Moovs review limo"
- "Moovs app driver experience"
- "Moovs problems issues"
- G2, Capterra, TrustPilot reviews
- Facebook groups for limo/black car operators

### 5. Company Stability
- When was Moovs founded?
- Funding history?
- Customer count?
- Will they be around in 3 years?

### 6. Limitations & Red Flags
- What CAN'T Moovs do?
- Scalability concerns?
- Reporting gaps?
```
